Since May of 2022, I have tried to go to at least one concert a month, up to as many as one concert per week. I have always loved listening to music and I have no idea why I ended up not going to concerts until so late in my life.

Each concert (the full list of which and venues can be found in the Timeline) typically has 2-3 artists, of which I rate those I remember.

A few other things to note:

  1. Most of the artists I have only seen live once. If this is not the case, it will be noted in paratheses the number of times I have seen them live.
  2. In some concerts, either the venue or the audience detracted enough from the experience to lower them a tier. This is noted by a small downward arrow (↓) next to the artist.
  3. If the venue or audience significantly detracted enough from the experience, this will be noted by a double small downward arrow (↓↓) next to the artist.
  4. If an artist has a comma in the name, their name will be quoted.
